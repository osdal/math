the Path of Bliss

Though worshiping Sri Krishna appears religious, it transcends conventional beliefs, as it forgoes both fear and the pursuit of bhukti (enjoyment) and mukti (salvation). Vaishnava-dharma or jiva-dharma embodies the soul's eternal bond with Krishna, founded on beauty and compassion. Here, you can explore this profound spiritual path and deepen your understanding of the timeless connection between the soul and Krishna.
